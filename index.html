About

Evelyn Evelyn is a musical duo formed by Amanda Palmer and Jason Webley. According to the fictional backstory described by Palmer and Webley, the duo consists of conjoined twin sisters, Evelyn and Evelyn Neville, who were discovered in 2007 by Palmer and Webley. The twins are actually portrayed by Palmer and Webley, dressed in connected bodysuits. The duo released their debut album in 2010.

The opening track, Evelyn Evelyn, sets the stage for the album, delving into the convergence of all challenges faced by the twins. It melts down into an argument on the sisters' shared birthday. The essence of the song is the yearning for individuality, a desire to exist in separate worlds that is only but a dream for the intertwined sisters.
